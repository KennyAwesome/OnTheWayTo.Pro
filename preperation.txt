Those who might feel overwhelmed and drowning in chaos might appreciate the following words:
"Creativity is the ability to introduce order into the randomness of nature."
Those who seek to understand "order" might appreicate the following proverb:
"Order is half of the rent."
Those who are creative might wonder, what would be the other half would be, 
if it was not the obvious implication of money.
One might dare to guess that it might be be stability,
if stability would be defined as the execution of order.
One might then be able to define order as the "knowledge of the patterns resulting in stability".
One might find one reason for it given the natural inclination of the organ called "brain" to recognize patterns.
One might then be able to define stablity as the absence of chaos.
One might then be able to define chaos through the absense of that knowledge or as "whatever remains for which a pattern cannot be recognized yet".
Those who wonder about the utility of such brain functions might appreciate the following realization:
"Life can only be understood backwards; but it must be lived forwards."
In order to live forwards there is a necessity to confront the "unknown".
One might appreciate the privilege to have survived so far without such effort.
Those might expereince a feeling of stagnation and boredom.
Those might appreciate the following words:
"The Daoist answer is right on the line, where you have one foot in order so that you’re fairly stable, and you have another foot in chaos so that new and interesting and compelling and transforming things are happening to you."
Those who are overwhelmed with the infinite possibilities for transformation might appreciate these words:
"Confidence comes from being prepared."
One might find a clue how to prepare in the following realization:
"Strategy is not the consequence of planning, but the opposite: its starting point."
Those who seek to understand strategy might appreciate these words:
"Tactics is knowing what to do, when there is something to do.
Strategy is knowing what to do when there is nothing to do."
Those who seek to understand tactics might appreciate the wisdom in the following proverb:
"If you want to go fast, go alone.
If you want to go far, go together."
Those who have go alone might appreciate these words:
"I'm not heartless.
I've just learned how to use my heart less."
Those who seek to go far might appreciate these words:
"Strategy is choice. Strategy means saying no to certain kinds of things.
Those who seek to understand what things to say no to might appreciate these words:
"The more we value things outside our control, the less control we have."
Those who seek to find whatever is in one's control might appreciate the following limited enumeration for a possible cause of death:
- Solar Flares:
	- Make electronics EMP proof
	- Have spare parts inside a EMP proof casing
	- Improving means of prediction through observation of cycles
- Black out / power outage:
	- Renewable or sustainable power sources
	- Fortification of existing power grid
	- Efforts to sustain offgrid life style
- Climate change and weather extremes:
	- Less reckless consumer behaviour
	- More investment in sustainable innovations
	- More research in safe places to live
	- Fully autarkic hydroponic agricultural systems
	- Working on an artsylum
- War:
	- Education of wisdom enabling those who seek war to agree on the necessity of world peace.
	- Better means for resource distribution
	- Better process or improvement of operation of governments
- Technoglogy:
	- Education about cyber security, artificial intelligence and technology.
	- Creation of automated defense mechanism
- Declining birthrate:
	- Education about factors for infertility
	- Increasing financial support and incentive for caregivers (e.g. UBI (universal basic income),
	- Education, therapy and volunterring for caregivers.
	- Preservation of genetic diversity through donation of cells for procreation.
	- Plan B: Artificial womb or cloning.
- Pandemic:
	- Education and research.
	- Maintaing and increasing means to produce means for medical care
	- Financial support to distribute such products to those in need.
- Asteroid:
	- Longterm observation of celestial bodies
	- Innovation in space travel to counter act them or to escape it.
- Suicide:
	- Seek the help of a health care professional.
