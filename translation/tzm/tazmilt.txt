Aselkin:
Ayagi ur d taneggarut n umaru.
Amaru ira ad isellm i umddakkel,
wenna t-iɛawnen ad ifhem cwiṭ ktar tamsalt n tudert.

Yezmer ad yaf yiwen ixdem abrid iwakken ad d-yessuffeɣ
akk iḥulfan-is imeqqranen
i yezmer ad d-ffɣen yal taswiɛt.
Yezmer daɣen ad yili win ara yafen
"ssebba" i yiḥulfan-agi imeqqranen.
Ma yella "ssebba-yagi" d aḥulfu n wul s tidet,
ur yezmir ara ad yini ɣef yiman-is d anaẓur,
ma ur yeɛriḍ ara ad d-yessuffeɣ akk iḥulfan ur d-neffiɣ ara.
Ma yufa yiwen iman-is weḥd-s,
yezmer ad yaf azal deg tedukli.
Anamek n "tadukli" yezmer ad yemgarad seg umdan ɣer wayeḍ,
maca ayen yeǧǧan talsa tedder ar tura d ayen fessusen am
tajaḍt i zzin wiyaḍ s ucrak n tisit
s yiwet n tneflit n "tayri":
"Aoi"
Iwakken anaẓur ad yili d amddakkel,
ilaq ad twalin daɣen ad ttqadaren ayen ixdem.
I wid irran ad rren afus i tedukli yellan,
zemren ad qadren tiwisi
n tuzzna n wayen xedmen i yimdukal-nniḍen.
Nezmer kan ad nessaram ad naf anaẓur i nezmer ad t-nini d amddakkel,
maca i wid ur t-nesɛi ara
tazlɣa-yagi tezmer ad tenfaɛ:
"Akk nekkni deg ubrid ɣer Unamur."
