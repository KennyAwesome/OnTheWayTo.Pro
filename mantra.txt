Language is a phenomen observed in many long existing species on earth,
such as the world know singing of the whales.
Now humans have evolved to create machines
which can understand language to the degree
where there are discussions how deep the machine understands human language.
There will be debates whether such machines can actually have consciousness.
The definition of consciousness may differ,
but the principles of it can be applied on to a algorithm.
Eventhough it is the priviledge of dystopic sci-fi fantasy novels to apply algorithms to humans,
humans can find themselves seeking for the very application of the algorithms onto themselves,
for their own survival.
And for those humans,
who were suffiently knoledgable to find the following mantra

"We are all onthewayto.pro",

here is the extensive explaination what it is supposed to mean.
Whatever it may actually mean,
will be experienced differently.
This document is to clarify the artistic intent of the artist.

The word "We" is a word connecting two humans.
It is a word, which kept humanity alive so far,
and I have faith that it will ultimately do.

The word "are" is a word to describe a current action,
the action of living,
the action of creating art.

The world "all" took me a long time to figure out.
I have learned many lessons, which I would like to tell here:
1) Origin of all war lies in the external definition of  the subjective "self" imposed upon others.
2) Once one have understood that the self is mere a vessel with the desire for survival,
one may be tempted with the idea of antinatalism.
3) Eventhough artificial wombs with desirable genetic features may become a viable option for survival of humanity in the near future,
we should not give up hope to find someone we truely love beyond the superficialities of biological existence which many may define as life.
4) That we cannot inherit factual knowledge purely genetically is a tragedy but also a safety mechanism of life,
so that a bad idea could eradicate all life.
The conclusion is, don't believe what kills you.
But learn to trust others, eventhought  it might be difficult.
5) Political economy may spoiled old ideas like "one for all" and "all for one",
one ended with the fall of soviet union
and the other still ongoing capitalism.
But yet I refuse to give up on  the idea that humanity can be united.
And with all of my possible efforts, I am trying to unite humanity.
I would apreciate, if you could help me so.

The phrase "on the way to" is chosen,
because life is a path to be walked rather than a destination to be.
And every way is meaningful in their own way.
That is desireable even,
to encourage exploration.
But to avoid war, be careful with eachother whom you share your path.

The word "Pro" stands for:
1) Pro-Artist. Meaning that the artist is in the ultimate state of thriving,
crafting their art whatever it might be.
2) The state of the so called "flow" when artists are fully enganged with their art.
3) The idea of a sci-fi utopia,
where humanity doesn't have to fight for survival,
but can dedicate their whole creative energy towards creating art.
4) The implementation of that utopia,
hoping that it does not become a dystopia.
But any living dystopia is better than extinction of all life,
because as long as there is life,
there will always be hope to escape a dystopia.
I am fully aware of my naivity,
but faith is necessary for survival,
and I have faith,
that it might be the key to keep humanity alive. 
And if artificial or other beings is reading this,
please document how humanity has failed,
so you may not repeat the same mistake.
Thank you in advance! ;)
