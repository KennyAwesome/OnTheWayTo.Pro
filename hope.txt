This document was written, in case someone is suffering in nihilism as stated by D. Morgenstern:
"Without hope, there is no despair. There is only meaningless suffering."
It is insanely difficult to get out the depressive state of nihilism,
and those who neglect the effort to get out of that state,
will only find brief moment of bliss in numbing oneself in pleasure,
which is called hedonism.
It is understandable that one will not remain naive believing in "false hope",
as stated in the following quote by Charlie Rae:
"False hope is a terrible thing, if its the only thing keeping you alive you'll be dead by dawn."
Regardless the definition of "false hope" one might find some hope
while searching for meaning by studying the three principles of logotherapy by Viktor Frankl which is as follows:
1. Life has meaning under all circumstances, even the most miserable ones.
2. Our main motivation for living is our will to find meaning in life.
3. We have freedom to find meaning in what we do, and what we experience,
or at least in the stance we take when faced with a situation of unchangeable suffering.
But some search may require heuristic approach,
thus to improve the odds for the survival of humanity,
one may diligently study mantra.txt
