One may attribute the title of "encouragement"
to the words one might have subconsciously seeking to
find back to the original strength of human as a species.
In that case one might appreciate the words by Friedrich Nietzsche:
"The strength of a person's spirit would then
be measured by how much 'truth' he could tolerate,
or more preciesely, to what extent he needs to have it diluted,
disguised, sweetened, muted, falsified."
For those who wants to prepare to tolerate the truth,
the following "Codes Of Honor" of a fictional tribe called "Mandalorians"
might come in handy:

"STRENGTH is life,
for the strong
have the right to rule.

HONOR is life,
for with no honor
one may as well be dead.

LOYALTY is life,
for without one's clan
one has no purpose.

DEATH is life,
one should die
as they have lived.

This is the Way."
