Some people may try to justify war by saying:
"The only excuse for war is that
we may live in peace unharmed." (Marcus Tullius Cicero)
But the essence of justice is vengence.
The only just atonement is to restore the original state,
which is usually impossible.
This dispair may trigger a series of negative emotion in humans,
which will end up in resentment, if untreated.
The ultimate treatment will result in forgiveness exemplified in this quote:
"Forgiveness is accepting the apology
you will never receive." (Shawne Duperon)
Someone has already come to that same conclusion:
"Those who are at war, are not at peace with themselves." (Willam Hazlitt)
To be at peace is a way of being as it has been already stated:
"We cannot have peace, if we are only concerned with peace.
War is not an accident. It is the logical outcome of a certain way of life.
If we want to attack war, we have to attack the way of life." (A. J. Muste)

There are as many ways of life, as there are individuals,
but as long as they work towards a common goal, they will be united.
This work requires understanding, which is in essence wisdom:
"Man has three ways of acting wisely.
First, on meditation; that is the noblest.
Secondly, on imitation; that is the easiest.
Thirdly, on experience; that is the bitterest."
(Confuzius)
Humanity has so far tried with experience,
thus a lot of them are bitter and resentful.
Some religions has tried with meditation,
but it ended up in ideological war.
So the only way left is through imitation, 
because not everyone has always the capacity for understanding.
In the age of weaponization of misinformation,
one might feel lost in the search for truth.
So we are at mercy of those
who genuinely cares for us with benevolence.
True care requires dedication and skill for extensive a research.
In essence, "The Ultimate Art" is an solution approach
to prevent war by caring and helping oneself and each other
with the best of ones abilities and resources, by defining it as art.
If it doesn't succeed due to its lack of maturity
caused by the limitation of resources,
the only thing left what we can do,
is to leave words of wisdom about the following mantra
for the next generation of humanity:
