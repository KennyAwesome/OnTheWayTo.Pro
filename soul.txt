Charles Bukowski once said:
"Understand me. I'm not like an ordinary world.
I have my madness,
I live in another dimension and 
I do not have time for things that have no soul."
One thus may ponder what that entity called "soul" may be.
It is immeasurable with the state of the art scientific equipment,
but yet its existence is undeniable
given the inhumane experients in human history.
One might find peace in the teaching of gurus specialized in metaphysics.
Others might find peace in the teaching of science fiction such as
"Ghost in the Shell" by Masamune Shirō.
If one is truely searching for one's own soul,
one may appriciate the advice that
one might find it during mindful activities,
such as meditation, workouts or
activities which requires balance,
such as bouldering, surfing or slacklining.
