while trying to minimize one's own suffering
as written in buddhist scriptures.

there used to be an extreme practice
called "living buddha" which is to be
voluntary burried alive with only a hole
to breathe which ended in death by starvation,
which scientists refer as "suicide".

a life without involuntary starvation.

a plant based diet for longetivity as mentioned
in the book "How Not to Die".

suffering from involuntary starvation might be reach
the presumably "rational" conclusion
that suicide might be a way to ease one's own suffering.

of "rationality" in order to stay alive:
"I think life's an irrational obsession."

other ways to help those who are suffering.

maintain that obsession to life voluntary as "art".

aprreciate these words:
"Until your mission is an obsession,
nothing will change in your life."

"I'm on a mission to help people grow and give."
