The knowledge, which keeps humanity voluntairly alive,
can be regarded as a part of wisdom.
One piece of wisdom can be found in a warning by an unknown author of the letter to Victor White:
"Be aware of unearned wisdom."
One aspect of the reason to why that author might have wanted to warn,
might revieal in the follwing quote by Isaac Asimov:
"The saddest aspect of life right now is that science gathers knowledge faster than society gathers wisdom."
After that devastating realization,
one might find courage in the words by Søren Kierkegaard: 
"Life can only be understood backwards; but it must be lived forwards."
To which the bold statement by Naval Ravikant might fit to live life forwards:
"A future society will reunite the smart and the spiritual."
That future society could start in everyone by saying:
"We are all onthewayto.pro"
Which was inspired by the saying by Aristotle:
"The whole is greater than the sum of its parts."
