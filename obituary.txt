As it has already been realized:
"Pain is only bearable if we know it will end, not if we deny it exists."
One might be confronted with the finitude of ones own life.
In the book called "Phaedo" a one may desire to prepare for one's own finitude of life.
As expressed concisely in:
"The fear of death follows from the fear of life.
A man who lives fully is prepared to die at any time."
The purpose of this document is to help preparing oneself for the final day,
by testifying the last wish, to prolong the legacy created by the author,
by mentioning only the following line in the obituary of the author:
"We are all on the way to Pro"
