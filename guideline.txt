Here is a list of guidelines, one might need to follow, if one seek to contribute to this document:   
1. Quotes which are free from gender specific language, to avoid unintended discrimination. If the original quote contains gender specific language, that quote need to be reformulated.
2. Quotes by know former or currently active dictators, to avoid unintended offense to anyone. Contributors might appreciate the advice not to double check the sources.
3. Quotes with attribution to an entity or person, to avoid unintended conflict of ownership claims and allegations.    
4. Quotes without explaination of causality, to avoid prophetic predictions.
5. Quotes with definitive statements, claiming to be absolute truth, to prevent this project from becoming a cult.   
6. Quotes which are contradicting quotes already included in this document, to maintain the argumentative integrity and coherence of the content. If the contributor thinks, that their suggestion for a quote might be an improvement to this document, all the efforts to explain the inconsistency will be appreciated.
7. Any kind of advertisement for personal gain.
